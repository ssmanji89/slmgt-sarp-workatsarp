Elevate Your Business at Summit at Renaissance Park

Prime Retail Opportunities in Houston’s Dynamic Market

Discover a rare opportunity to lease 17,000+ square feet of Class A retail space—designed to integrate seamlessly with a premier residential community and set in one of Houston’s most promising districts.

About the Opportunity

Summit at Renaissance Park isn’t just an upscale residential destination—it also offers a sophisticated retail environment built to support and enhance the local community. The retail component is purposefully designed to attract businesses that provide services, dining, and lifestyle experiences for both residents and professionals in the area.

Retail Space Features

  • 17,000+ Square Feet of Flexible Space: Modern design and open floor plans accommodate a variety of retail formats.
  • Premium Finishes & State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: Ensure a sophisticated, contemporary look that appeals to high-end customers.
  • High Visibility & Accessibility: Located near the northeast intersection of Beltway 8 and I-45 North, the space benefits from exceptional traffic and exposure.
  • Integrated Mixed-Use Environment: With a thriving residential community and adjacent office campuses, enjoy an immediate, built-in customer base.

A Strategic Location for Growth

  • Robust Foot Traffic: From local residents, professionals, and visitors drawn by nearby amenities.
  • Proximity to Key Employment Centers: Including CityNorth’s office campus and major transit routes, ensuring a constant flow of potential customers.
  • Economic Vitality & Growth Potential: The area is recognized for its dynamic revitalization efforts and increasing demand for quality retail services.

Community & Retail Synergy

Leasing retail space at Summit at Renaissance Park means more than securing a location—it means joining an ecosystem that values quality, design, and connectivity. The curated mix of retail, dining, and services is intended to complement the residential environment, fostering built-in customer engagement and collaborative community dynamics.

Welcome to Summit at Renaissance Park, an exclusive apartment community located in Harris County within the 77060 ZIP Code and served by the Aldine Independent attendance zone. Our community offers modern living with state-of-the-art smart security, remote control access, and innovative energy-efficient designs.
