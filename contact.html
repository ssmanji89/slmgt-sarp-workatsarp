Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What size spaces are available at 12121 Greenspoint Dr?

Currently, we have approximately 17,368 SF of retail space available. This space can be divided to accommodate different business needs. Contact our leasing team for specific details about available configurations.

What are the lease terms?

We offer flexible lease terms including short-term, standard (3-5 years), and long-term options. Our leasing team can work with you to find terms that best suit your business needs.

Can I customize the space?

Yes, we offer tenant improvement options to help customize the space to meet your specific requirements. Our team can provide information about improvement allowances and available options.

How do I schedule a tour?

You can schedule a tour by calling our leasing team at (123) 456-7890, sending an email to leasing@workatsarp.com, or completing the contact form on this page.

What amenities are available at the property?

12121 Greenspoint Dr offers modern amenities including ample parking, security systems, high-speed connectivity infrastructure, and energy-efficient features. Visit our About page for more details.

How soon can I move in after signing a lease?

Move-in timelines depend on the specific space and any required tenant improvements. Our leasing team can provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific situation.
