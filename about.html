Property Overview

12121 Greenspoint Dr is a premier commercial property located in the thriving Greater Greenspoint area of Houston. The property offers approximately 17,368 SF of retail space designed to meet the needs of modern businesses.

With its strategic location, modern amenities, and flexible space options, 12121 Greenspoint Dr provides an ideal environment for businesses looking to establish or expand their presence in a dynamic commercial district.

The property is managed by ZG Companies, a respected name in commercial real estate known for quality properties and exceptional management services.
